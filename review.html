Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our structural methodologies, timelines, fees, and operational capabilities.

Our process consists of five distinct phases: Consultation, Concept Design, Design Development, Sourcing & Procurement, and Construction Administration. We handle everything from spatial planning and 3D modeling to the final styling, ensuring a flawless execution.
Timeline varies depending on the scale and layout of the project. A single room redesign generally takes 4 to 8 weeks, whereas a complete residential home renovation ranges from 4 to 8 months. We provide detailed timeline milestones prior to project kickoff.
Yes, we offer both on-site design services internationally and comprehensive virtual design services. For remote projects, we conduct initial surveys, supply detailed technical packages, and collaborate closely with your local General Contractors.
We offer a complimentary initial 30-minute discovery call. Detailed physical home consultations (2 hours) are charged at a flat rate, during which our lead designer maps spaces, assesses style guides, and drafts initial concept blueprints.
We love incorporating personal heirlooms and existing sentimental pieces. During consultation, we review items to evaluate structural integrity and stylistic alignment, blending legacy pieces with custom-sourced furniture for a harmonious finish.
